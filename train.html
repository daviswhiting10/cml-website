Group Training Sessions

Every session is led by both an offensive and defensive coach, giving athletes position-specific attention within a high-energy group environment. Sessions are intentionally kept small to ensure every player gets meaningful reps, direct feedback, and a training experience that actually translates to the field. This is not a camp. This is structured, high-level development — the same approach used to train Division I athletes, brought to Central Maryland.

What We Bring

Footwork & Athleticism

Speed, agility, and movement mechanics built for lacrosse. We train athletes to move efficiently on the field — getting to spots faster, winning footraces, and creating separation.

Lacrosse IQ

Decision-making, field awareness, and understanding the game at a high level. We put athletes in live-rep environments where they have to read and react — not just run drills.

Individual Development

Each athlete is assessed individually. We identify what's holding them back and build a clear path forward — whether the goal is making the high school team or getting in front of college coaches.

Strength & Nutrition

Our coaches hold certifications in strength & conditioning and nutrition. We can provide guidance on off-field habits that support performance, recovery, and long-term athletic development.
