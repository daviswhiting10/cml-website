About

Central Maryland Lax is a premier lacrosse training community serving the Columbia, Silver Spring, and Frederick areas and communities in between. Our mission is simple: bring high-level, D1-informed training to athletes who may not have had access to it. Whether you're preparing for your first high school season or positioning yourself for a collegiate career, we meet you where you are and build from there.
