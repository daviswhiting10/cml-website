About

Central Maryland Lax is a premier lacrosse training community serving the Columbia, Silver Spring, and Frederick areas — and the underserved communities in between. Our mission is simple: bring high-level, D1-informed training to athletes who may not have had access to it. Whether you're preparing for your first high school season or positioning yourself for a collegiate career, we meet you where you are and build from there.

Schedule

Sessions run Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Specific times to be confirmed — check back or subscribe to updates.

Locations

We operate out of 3 field locations in Howard County. Primary locations are River Hill High School and Cedar Lane Park. Alternate location is Glenelg High School. Preferred location may be requested.
