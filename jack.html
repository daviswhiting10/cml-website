Jack Simon

Head Instructor

Bio

Former D1 lacrosse player at High Point University with a B.S. in Exercise Science (Kinesiology) and a Computer Science minor — currently pursuing an MBA in Data Analytics. Jack blends elite-level athletic experience with the science of human performance and a data-driven approach to training, giving athletes a coach who understands the body, the game, and the numbers behind real results.

Jack has a passion for the game of lacrosse and developing student-athletes to their greatest potential. With years of highly-competitive experience and great mentors he has decided to pass on knowledge for the better developement of future generations
